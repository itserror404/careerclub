Morgan Stanley - Company Overview

About Morgan Stanley

Morgan Stanley is a leading global financial services firm providing investment banking, securities, wealth management, and investment management services. With offices in more than 41 countries, the Firm's employees serve clients worldwide, including corporations, governments, institutions, and individuals. Morgan Stanley's purpose is to help clients achieve their financial goals and make a positive impact on their lives and their communities.

Our Business Divisions

Morgan Stanley operates through four main business divisions:

  • Institutional Securities
  • Investment Management
  • Wealth Management
  • Bank Resource Management

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Morgan Stanley is committed to promoting sustainable finance and supporting the transition to a low-carbon economy. We are focused on integrating environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors into our business practices and investment strategies, and we collaborate with clients to develop innovative financing solutions for sustainable projects and initiatives.

Our Global Presence

Morgan Stanley operates in more than 41 countries across the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia-Pacific, allowing us to provide comprehensive financial solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our global presence enables us to help drive economic growth, create jobs, and contribute to local communities around the world.

Our Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion

At Morgan Stanley, we believe that diversity and inclusion are essential for our success. We strive to create an inclusive workplace where all employees feel valued, respected, and have equal opportunities to succeed. Our commitment to diversity extends to our clients, suppliers, and communities. We actively work to promote diversity in our workforce, supplier base, and the communities we serve.

Awards and Recognition

Morgan Stanley has received numerous awards and recognition for its commitment to clients, employees, and communities. Some of our recent accolades include:

  • World's Best Bank for Wealth Management (Euromoney)
  • Top Investment Bank in the World (Global Finance)
  • Best Global Equity House (Euromoney)
  • Best Bank for Financing (The Banker)
  • Best Places to Work for LGBTQ+ Equality (Human Rights Campaign)

Careers at Morgan Stanley

Morgan Stanley offers numerous opportunities for talented and motivated individuals to join our team. We value our employees and provide them with the resources, training, and support needed to succeed and grow professionally. We offer competitive compensation, comprehensive benefits, and a collaborative work environment where employees can thrive.
