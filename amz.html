Amazon - Company Overview

About Amazon

Amazon is a multinational technology company that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It is one of the world's largest online retailers and a prominent cloud services provider. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, Amazon has grown to become a global leader in the technology industry and a major force in the retail space.

Services and Products

Amazon offers a wide range of services and products to meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and institutions:

  • Online Retail
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Amazon Prime Video
  • Amazon Music
  • Kindle E-readers and E-books
  • Alexa Voice Assistant

Industries

Amazon serves clients in various industries, including:

  • Retail and E-commerce
  • Cloud Computing
  • Media and Entertainment
  • Consumer Electronics
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Logistics and Supply Chain

Awards and Recognitions

> Amazon has received numerous awards and recognitions for its commitment to quality, innovation, and diversity, including:

  • World's Most Valuable Brand (Forbes)
  • Top Companies for Women Technologists (AnitaB.org)
  • Best Companies for Multicultural Women (Working Mother)
  • Best Companies to Work For (Fortune)

Careers at Amazon
