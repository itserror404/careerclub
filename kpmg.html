KPMG - Company Overview

About KPMG

KPMG is a global network of professional firms providing Audit, Tax, and Advisory services. We operate in 146 countries and have more than 227,000 professionals working in member firms around the world. KPMG is committed to providing quality services to help organizations navigate through business challenges and opportunities in today's complex environment.

Services

KPMG offers a wide range of services to meet the needs of businesses, governments, and not-for-profit organizations:

  • Audit & Assurance
  • Tax & Legal
  • Advisory
  • Enterprise
  • Deal Advisory

Industries

KPMG serves clients in various industries, including:

  • Banking and Capital Markets
  • Consumer and Retail
  • Energy and Natural Resources
  • Government and Public Sector
  • Healthcare and Life Sciences
  • Industrial Manufacturing
  • Infrastructure
  • Insurance
  • Real Estate
  • Technology, Media, and Telecommunications

Awards and Recognitions

KPMG has received numerous awards and recognitions for its commitment to quality, innovation, and diversity, including:

  • World's Most Attractive Employers (Universum)
  • Top Global Employer (Stonewall)
  • Best Place to Work for Disability Inclusion (Disability:IN and AAPD)
  • Most JUST Companies in America (Forbes and JUST Capital)

Careers at KPMG

KPMG is dedicated to attracting and retaining top talent, offering employees rewarding career opportunities and a supportive work environment. We provide competitive compensation packages, comprehensive benefits, and extensive training and development programs to help our employees excel in their careers.
