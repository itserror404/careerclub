Goldman Sachs - Company Overview

About Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment banking, securities, and investment management firm. Founded in 1869 by Marcus Goldman and Samuel Sachs, the firm has its headquarters in New York City and provides a wide range of financial services to corporations, financial institutions, governments, and individuals.

Services

Goldman Sachs offers a diverse range of services, including:

  • Investment Banking
  • Global Markets
  • Asset Management
  • Consumer & Wealth Management
  • Corporate Treasury

Industries

Goldman Sachs serves clients in various industries, such as:

  • Financial Services
  • Technology
  • Healthcare
  • Energy
  • Real Estate
  • Consumer Products

Awards and Recognitions

Goldman Sachs has received numerous awards and recognitions for its commitment to quality, innovation, and diversity, including:

  • World's Best Investment Bank (Euromoney)
  • Top Investment Bank in the Americas (Financial Times)
  • Best Global Investment Research (Institutional Investor)
  • Top Companies for Diversity (Forbes)

Careers at Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs is committed to attracting and retaining top talent, offering employees rewarding career opportunities and a supportive work environment. The firm provides competitive compensation packages, comprehensive benefits, and extensive training and development programs to help employees excel in their careers.
